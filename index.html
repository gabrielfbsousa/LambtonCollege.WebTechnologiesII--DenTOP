Introducing DenTOP

Dentist Timetable Organizer Program



A web system created for you not to worry about the scheduling of your dentist company.



WELCOME TO THE FUTURE


NO MORE TEXTBOOKS...Textbooks are on the past! with DenTOP, you leave your pen and paper away, and enter your dentist business into the digital era!


DenTOP is a web system, which helps you to manage and schedule appointments for your patients quickly. But it is not only this: you can manage and review the appointments from each one of your customers. More than that, you can manage your dentists, review their schedule, view the content of the appointments and much more!

SATISFACTION FOR YOUR TEAM...


WITH DenTOP, YOUR DENTISTS GET TOOLS TO ORGANIZE THEIR SCHEDULE. Before leaving home, with a single view on the DenTOP system, they can plan how will be their day!


More than that: the DenTOP web system can help them to follow along all the development of the patient's treatments, due to the possibility to review the appointments by patient. The dentists can have a complete report of the previous meetings, with description and pictures. With more control over their schedule and appointments, your team have tools to adjust themselves and offer more!

... AND FOR YOUR CUSTOMERS


THEIR DENTAL NEEDS ATTENDED ON ONE RING. DenTOP simplifies the way your patients will make appointments. On less than one minute, with a single call, they grant a seat with the best dentists.


DenTOP makes the scheduling process completely easy. When calling to your dentist, an assistant will have the needed toos to quickly find a spot for your customer. Needs special treatment? no problem: our system qualifies dentists by their skills. Just need "the same as always"? That's good too: by reviewing his/her historic, the assistant will find your preferred doctors. See more on our step-by-step presented on the Features section.
